Tips for succeful mentor-student relationship

  1. Design and implement—with input from the participant—an individualized developmental plan.
  2. Provide a nurturing and stimulating intellectual atmosphere.
  3. Leave instructions for your participant when you are unavailable, and designate a substitute mentor to serve in your absence.
  4. Encourage them to write a report on the research or technical project being conducted during the appointment period.
