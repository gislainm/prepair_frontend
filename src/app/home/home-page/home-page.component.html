OUR VISION

Potential is equally distributed; resources are not. PrePair aims to provide resources necessary for college STEM students to succeed in their careers through community based mentorship. Our movement priorities relationships and fuels opportunity for STEM students who needs mentorship and guidance.

OUR MISSION

PrePair's mission is to make mentorship and career guidance widely available for STEM students in college especially first generation students and students from marginalized communities.
